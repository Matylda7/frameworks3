The app is available for download on the Apple App Store for iOS devices and the Google Play Store for Android devices.

To change your password/username, open the app and navigate to your profile settings. Look for the options to 'Change Password' or 'Edit Profile.' Follow the on-screen instructions to update your information.

Creating an event on Neighbours in Nature is easy! Just open the app and navigate to your profile, click on 'myGroups' and then click on 'Create Event'.

To cancel an event, log in to your Neighbours in Nature account and go to your 'MyEvents' page. Find the event you want to cancel and follow the on-screen instructions.

To find all the green areas in your town, go to your town Home page and click on the map under the Town name.
